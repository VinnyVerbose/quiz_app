   ↓
render()


TAKING QUIZ
   ↓
renderQuestion()
   ↓
getCurrentQuestion()

show question + answers

answer clicked
   ↓
handleAnswerSelected()
   ↓
recordAnswer()
   ↓
if normal/review mode:
      advanceQuiz()

   ↓

No

if quizMode === "review"
      screen = "results"
else

   ↓
render()

renderResults()

show:

- review buttons

startReview(filter)

isReviewing = true

   ↓
render()



REVIEWING
   ↓
renderQuestion()
   ↓
getCurrentQuestion()
   ↓
uses:

renderAnswer()

   ↓
show highlights
   ↓

   ↓
